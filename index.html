Who is Kartograph for?

Kartograph is created with the specific needs of designers and data journalists in mind, or in short, for people who want to tell data-driven stories using maps.

Features

  • Free choice of map projection
  • Choropleth maps for grown-ups
  • Dot grid maps
  • 3D map projection
  • Symbol maps
  • Hell, it even works in Internet Explorer 7 (at least 90%)
