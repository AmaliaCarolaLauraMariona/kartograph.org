Why Kartograph?

Kartograph is a new framework for building interactive vector maps. It was created with the specific needs of designers and data journalists in mind, or, in short, for people who want to tell data-driven stories using maps.

In contrast to many other available mapping frameworks, Kartograph frees you from the Spherical Mercator projection, allowing you to choose whatever map projection is fitting your needs.

Features

  • Data-vis-friendliness, including choropleth, dot grid and symbol maps
  • Free choice of map projection (even 3D)
  • Runs 100% stand-alone
  • Down-grades to Internet Explorer 7
