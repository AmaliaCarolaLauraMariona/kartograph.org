What’s in the box?

Here’s a short list of the wonderful features included in Kartograph.

  • 13 ready to use map projections
  • Choropleth maps for grown-ups
  • 3D map projection
  • Symbol maps
  • Hell, it even works in Internet Explorer 7 (at least 90%)

And what's not in it?

  • Sorry, but no tile maps. If you really need street-level details in your map, try other great mapping frameworks.
